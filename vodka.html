Passion fruit martini

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe passion fruits (they should have a crinkly appearance), halved
  • 60ml vanilla vodka
  • 30ml passoa
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp sugar syrup
  • Prosecco to serve

  1. Scoop the seeds from one of the passion fruits into the glass of a cocktail shaker, add the vodka, passoa, lime juice and sugar syrup. Add a handful of ice and shake well, strain into 2 martini glasses, top up with prosecco, then add half a passion fruit to each. Serve immediately.

Waterloo sunsets

Ingredients


  • 200ml vanilla vodka (or add 1 tsp vanilla extract to 200ml plain vodka)
  • 100ml peach schnapps
  • 500ml chilled peach juice
  • chilled soda or lemonade, to fill
  • splash of grenadine
  • A few rasberries

  1. In a jug, mix vodka (or vanilla extract), with schnapps and juice. Divide between 8 glasses.

  2. Add a few ice cubes, fill completely with soda or lemonade, then gently pour grenadine into each, over a teaspoon down the inside of the glass so it settles at the bottom. Add raspberries, a straw for stirring and pass around!
